Range

Being one of the longest range Airbus aircraft the A333 has a range of 23:44 of flight time. That is 109,184 kg's of fuel.

Passenger Numbers

The Airbus A330-300 can hold a maximum of 440 passengers. That is 33,900 kg's.

MTOW and MLW

The MTOW of the Airbus A333 is 242,000 kg's. The MLW is 185,000

Interesting Information

The Airbus A333 was one of the most successful Airbus aircraft selling over 1000 units and still being in operation with multiple airlines. This aircraft can be found at almost every major airline in all continents of the world.
