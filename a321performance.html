Range

The Airbus A321 has a range of 09:46 of flight time. That is 25,244 kg's of fuel.

Passenger Numbers

The Airbus A321-200 can hold a maximum of 239 passengers. That is 18,400 kg's.

MTOW and MLW

The MTOW of the Airbus A321 is 93,800 kg's. The MLW is 77,800

Interesting Information

The Airbus A321-200 is the longest of the A321 non NEO family of aircraft. Being a high capacity further range aircraft it was used for longer routes.
