Range

The Airbus A320 has a range of 07:44 of flight time. That is 19,616 kg's of fuel.

Passenger Numbers

The Airbus A320-200 can hold a maximum of 175 passengers. That is 13,500 kg's.

MTOW and MLW

The MTOW of the Airbus A320 is 78,000 kg's. The MLW is 66,500

Interesting Information

The Airbus A320-200 was and still is the most popular of the A320 non NEO family. With over 4,752 aircraft ever being delivered.
