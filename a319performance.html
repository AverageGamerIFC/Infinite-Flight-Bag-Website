Range

The Airbus A319 has a range of 08:01 of flight time. That is 19,616 kg's of fuel.

Passenger Numbers

The Airbus A319-100 can hold a maximum of 136 passengers. That is 10,483 kg's.

MTOW and MLW

The MTOW of the Airbus A319 is 75,500 kg's. The MLW is 62,500

Interesting Information

The Airbus A319 has had over 1,400 aircraft ever delivered. It is still in operational service with a lot of airlines with the main operator being American Airlines.
