Range

Being the baby bus, the Airbus A318 has a range of 07:56 of flight time. That is 19,616 kg's of fuel.

Passenger Numbers

The Airbus A318-300 can hold a maximum of 136 passengers. That is 10,483 kg's.

MTOW and MLW

The MTOW of the Airbus A318 is 68,000 kg's. The MLW is 57,600

Interesting Information

The Airbus A318 is known for flying Speedbird 1. The iconic route from London City to New York City with a stop over in Shannon, Ireland.
